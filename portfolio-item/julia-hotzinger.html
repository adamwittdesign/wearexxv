  • THESIS TITLE Vively: Designing Mindfulness
  • PROBLEM How can interactive design and behavioral design facilitate mindfulness while working, increase productivity, and create better work habits?
  • DESCRIPTION Stress and limited physical activity are the biggest hindrances to health and happiness in the workplace. Demanding jobs that require hours sitting at a desk create unsuitable working conditions that lead to anxiety, headaches, and bodily pain. The solution is workplace well being. Vively is a application that will help people to work smarter and not harder. Using smart watches and fitness bands, Vively tracks your activity and stress level and nudges the user towards making better habits. By addressing the physical and mental aspects of our daily routine, Vively can create peace of mind and well being, greater focus and creativity and better habits.
