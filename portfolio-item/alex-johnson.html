Alex Johnson

When Alex is not geeking out about the latest and greatest within technology, you can find him playing sports or riding his motorcycle.

  • CATEGORY Human Centered Interface Design, UX/UI
  • WEBSITE alxdavid.com
  • THESIS TITLE Sense : A predictive heads up display (HUD) for motorcyclists
  • PROBLEM How can cognitive science and microinteractions be applied to interface design in a motorcycle helmet that provides riders with an enhanced experience of their environment?
  • DESCRIPTION My hypothesis is that a heads up display does have the right to exist and should exist within a motorcycle helmet. It would help in creating an enhanced user experience for the rider by understanding and applying the principles of cognitive science, microinteractions, gestalt theory, usability heuristics, and context to the design of the interface.
